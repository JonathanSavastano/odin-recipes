Egg in a hole

Description

Egg in a hole is a classic lazy breakfast option. All you really need is an egg, a piece of bread, and a spoon. This meal is perfect for anyone who wants a quick and easy breakfast solution.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 pieces of your favorite bread
  • salt and pepper
  • butter
  • Your favorite hot sauce

Steps

  1. Butter a pan on medium high
  2. Remove the middle portion of each piece of bread
  3. Put bread in pan
  4. Crack egg inside the hole in the bread
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. cook until firm and flippable
  7. Give that badboy a fliparoo
  8. Season other side
  9. Lather with hot sauce and serve
